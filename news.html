Alliance Medical Radiopharmacy Update:

NeuraCeq™ (FBB) is now produced and dispatched from Guildford twice weekly, on Monday and Friday.

Sodium Fluoride (NaF) is produced and dispatched from Keele, weekly on Wednesday.

Choline (FEC) continues to be produced and dispatched from Keele weekly on Thursday, and twice weekly, on Tuesday and Friday, from Sutton.

The Dinnington refurbishment is progressing with go live currently anticipated to be early next year.

The Sutton RPU refurbishment to commence once Dinnington RPU is fully operational in 2021, the final step in our refurbishment plan in order to future proof the supply of radio-isotopes.
